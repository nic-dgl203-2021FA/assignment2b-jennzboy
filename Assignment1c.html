Distillery Advert

I created this advert for an assignment in my DGL 112 Typography class. The purpose of this assignment was to create a wordmark for a distillery brand using negative space, and an advert with a persuasive message about the brands unique selling points.

For this assignment, I created the brand Crescent Moon Distillery, and used crescent moons in place of the o's in 'moon' to show use of negative space. The goal of the advert was to show that the distillery used locally sourced, organic ingredients in their liquor, and to showcase that with a refreshing cocktail illustration.

Magazine Cover

I created this magazine cover for an assignment in my DGL 112 Typography class. The purpose of this assignment was to create a magazine cover for a fictional magazine, with a focus on text, typography and composition.

For this assignment, I created the arts and culture magazine Too Good. The cover image is from Unsplash and was taken by Jon Tyson. I wanted Too Good magazine to feel very fun and vibrant, and I experimented with a bold and decorative font for the nameplate. The headlines feature articles about some of my favourite artists and musicians.

Magazine Impressum and Letter from the Editor

I created this magazine page for an assignment in my DGL 112 Typography class. The purpose of this assignment was to create an impressum (a list of all those who worked on the magazine) and letter from the editor as a continuation of the magazine cover assignment. This magazine page needed to have a strong coherent visual character that was consistent with the magazine cover.

For this assignment, I created the magazine page to have the same fun and vibrant aesthetic as the cover page, while also being functional. The letter from the editor briefly outlines what each of the articles on the cover are about.
